PRIVACY POLICY

Last Updated: December 2025

1. The Core Principle

At IDRISIUM, privacy is not a feature; it is the foundation. We believe that your data belongs to you, and you alone. Our applications are designed with an Offline-First architecture, meaning your personal data is processed and stored locally on your device.

2. Data Collection

We do not collect, sell, or rent your personal information to third parties. Any data generated within our apps (such as notes, gallery items, or health metrics) remains encrypted on your device.

3. Analytics & Diagnostics

To improve our services, we may collect anonymous, non-identifiable usage data (e.g., app crash reports). This data is strictly used for debugging and performance optimization and cannot be traced back to any individual user.

4. Third-Party Services

Some of our apps may utilize third-party APIs for specific features (e.g., maps). These interactions are minimized and anonymized wherever possible.
